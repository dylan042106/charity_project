About Us

Peace4Poppies is a profit free charity that receives donations
from generous beings help end Poppy suffering.

We fight poppy suffering by using your donations to
deploy rescue missions for abandoned and/or injured poppies.
Your donations also fund food, medicine, and shelter for rescued poppies.
